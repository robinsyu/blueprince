Welcome to Mt. Holly

Blue Prince is a genre-defying experience filled with mystery, strategy, and puzzles that weave together to create an unpredictable journey through a manor with shifting rooms. As the heir to Mt. Holly, you must explore its ever-changing halls while uncovering a past filled with intrigue and secrets.

Drafting Your Journey

Upon reaching a closed door in Mt. Holly, you decide what room appears behind it from a choice of three options. Each decision shapes your path as you navigate through the manor. Every door can reveal new and exciting chambers that contain their own unique challenges and secrets.

Every Step Counts

Your progress each day is shaped by the rooms you select to draft and the tools you find within them. Items in the game can be used in creative ways to fuel your exploration deeper into the house, allowing you to adopt unique strategies to combat the challenges that each day brings.

A History in the Making

As your journey takes you further into the mansion's depths, you start to discover that there is more lurking under the surface than a missing room. Investigate a past woven with the threads of blackmail, political intrigue, and the mysterious disappearance of a local children's book author.

Innovative Gameplay

Shifting Floor Plan

Each dawn brings a new configuration to Mt. Holly. The house's shapeshifting floor plan is a five-by-nine grid to be filled anew each day with rooms you draft.

Room Drafting System

When opening a door, choose from three random room options. The further you get from the entrance, the more likely you are to draw rarer rooms.

Resource Management

Manage keys, money, energy, and gems as you explore. Each room requires energy to enter, forcing strategic decisions about your exploration path.

Puzzle Solving

Encounter a variety of puzzles throughout the manor. Solve them to unlock new areas, gain permanent upgrades, and uncover the mysteries of Mt. Holly.

Enter The Manor

Explore Mt. Holly's calming cel-shaded art style and discover the unique character of each room.

Frequently Asked Questions

When was Blue Prince released?

What are the system requirements?

Minimum: Windows 10/11 64-bit, i5-2300/Ryzen 3 1200, 16GB RAM, NVIDIA GTX 1060/AMD RX580. Recommended: i5 4690K/Ryzen 5 1500x, GTX 1080/RX 5700. Storage: 6 GB available space.

How does the room system work?

The manor's rooms change position and content each dawn. Players must strategically choose which rooms to explore from a random choice of three options when opening doors, using tools found within to progress.
